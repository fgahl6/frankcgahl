Preserving Attribution and Accountability in AI-Scale Systems

Discover Artificial Intelligence, 2026 · DOI: 10.1007/s44163-026-01415-9

This article examines attribution collapse as a socio-technical problem in AI-scale systems and introduces BlockClaim as a minimal provenance framework for preserving attribution continuity and temporal priority without relying on centralized authority, enforcement mechanisms, or prior adjudication.

Keywords: Provenance · Attribution · AI-scale systems · Accountability · Socio-technical systems · Philosophy of technology
